About Mandar Bapat

A decade of advice built on trust, not commission.

Mandar Bapat began his career in retail banking in India before making Australia home in 2014. Since then he has built a career in Australian financial planning with a single focus — giving clients advice that genuinely serves their interests, not a product provider's agenda.

In 2024 Mandar founded Altruist Advisers Australia under his own Australian Financial Services Licence. No bank tells him what to recommend. No product provider influences his strategy. His only obligation is to the client in front of him.

Mandar specialises in comprehensive financial advice — superannuation, retirement planning, insurance, investments, and debt reduction — built around your specific situation and your real goals.

He has a particular focus on helping Perth's Indian and Marathi community navigate the Australian financial system. Having built his own financial life here as a migrant, he understands the unique challenges of building wealth in a new country.
